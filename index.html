About me

Since October 2020, I am a PhD student at Télécom Paris, in the department IDS (Image, Data and Signal), under the supervision of Stephan Clémençon.
My work focuses on spatial statistics, nonparametric estimation, and spatio-temporal Hawkes processes.

Prior to this, I graduated from Master M2 MVA at ENS Paris-Saclay.
You can find more about my background in my resume.
